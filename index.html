OSF SEMC Screening Criteria
2 or more + age 18 or older = Sepsis Alert:
-HR above 90 bpm
-RR above 22 — or PaCO2 below 32 — or mechanical ventilation
-SBP at or below 90 mmHg
-Temp above 100.4F or below 96.8F
-AMS or Decreased LOC
-Chief complaint suggestive of infection
-Hyperglycemia or Hypoglycemia
-Already treating infection

-Patient age and chief complaint
-All positive screening criteria

-POC Lactate value

-ETA to receiving facility
